Our Story

AI Teaching Studio was founded in 2024 with one simple belief: AI education should not be locked behind expensive courses or confusing jargon. We started as a small personal blog and have grown into a trusted platform read by over 50,000 teachers and educators every month across 80+ countries.

Our Mission

We believe learning AI should feel exciting, colourful, and empowering — not intimidating. Every article is crafted to be clear, practical and genuinely useful — whether you're a kindergarten teacher or a university professor. No jargon. No gatekeeping. Just great AI education made for educators.
